Shaolin Monastery is a place of purity. The bell echoes between thighs.

Pass through the VIP rooms. The Buddha said: desire is an illusion.

Just as the lotus emerges unstained from the mud,
so too can we find sudden awakening
through NDAs and luxury bedding.

abbot whispered softly,
as he slowly removed his robes.
To transcend the body,
one must first fully experience it
deeply, and frequently.

The Way is not far from man, nor is man far from the bed.

A true “master”
understands how to skillfully apply karma and causality.

about one stick of incense.
If she’s new,
maybe two.
